Narendra Modi

Political Details: Narendra Modi is an Indian politician who has been serving as the Prime Minister of India since May 2014. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and belongs to the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) - a Hindu nationalist organization in India.Modi's political career began in the early 1980s when he joined the BJP and quickly rose through the ranks. He served as the Chief Minister of the state of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014, where he gained attention for his governance style and economic policies.

As Prime Minister, Narendra Modi has implemented various initiatives and policies, including the "Make in India" campaign to promote manufacturing and job creation, the "Digital India" program to enhance digital infrastructure, and the "Swachh Bharat Abhiyan" (Clean India Mission) to improve sanitation and hygiene across the country.

He has also focused on foreign policy, strengthening relations with various countries and promoting India's interests on the global stage. Modi has been known for his dynamic leadership style and his ability to connect with the masses through social media.

Rahul Gandhi

Political Details: Rahul Gandhi is an Indian politician and a member of the Indian National Congress (INC) party. He comes from a prominent political family, as he is the son of former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi and the grandson of former Prime Minister Indira Gandhi. Rahul Gandhi has held various positions within the Indian National Congress party. He served as the Vice President of the party from 2013 to 2017 and was later elected as its President in 2017, a position he held until 2019.

Throughout his political career, Rahul Gandhi has been involved in numerous campaigns and initiatives. He has advocated for the rights of farmers, youth empowerment, and social justice. He has also spoken on issues such as corruption, women's safety, and economic development. Rahul Gandhi has contested several elections, both at the national and state levels, and has represented the Amethi constituency in the Lok Sabha (lower house of parliament) multiple times.He is a Member of Parliament (MP) representing the Wayanad constituency in the state of Kerala, India. In recent years, he has also been active in campaigning for the party in various state elections.

Arvind Kejriwal

Political Details:Arvind Kejriwal is an Indian politician and the current Chief Minister of Delhi. He is the founder and leader of the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP), a political party focused on anti-corruption and good governance.

Kejriwal began his career as a civil servant in the Indian Revenue Service but eventually resigned from his position to focus on social activism and political work. He gained prominence as an anti-corruption crusader during the India Against Corruption movement led by social activist Anna Hazare.

In 2012, Kejriwal formed the Aam Aadmi Party, which went on to achieve significant success in the 2013 Delhi Legislative Assembly elections. However, his first term as Chief Minister was short-lived, as he resigned after 49 days due to the failure to pass the Jan Lokpal Bill in the Delhi Assembly. In the subsequent elections held in 2015, the Aam Aadmi Party swept to power in Delhi with a historic mandate, winning 67 out of 70 seats in the Legislative Assembly. Kejriwal became the Chief Minister once again, and his government has focused on issues such as education, healthcare, water, and electricity reforms.

Kejriwal has been known for his populist policies and a direct style of governance, often interacting with the public through town hall meetings and social media. His government has introduced initiatives such as Mohalla Clinics (community health centers), free water supply, and subsidized electricity rates.
